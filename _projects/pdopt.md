## Overview

**PDOPT** is an open-source Python library developed during my PhD at Cranfield University. It implements a set-based design approach for exploring large, multi-objective design spaces in multidisciplinary aircraft design.

## Key Features

- Pareto front exploration with multi-objective optimisation
- Surrogate model integration (Gaussian Processes)
- Compatible with OpenMDAO-based disciplinary models
- Designed for hybrid-electric propulsion trade studies

## Technology Stack

`Python` · `NumPy` · `SciPy` · `scikit-learn` · `OpenMDAO` · `Matplotlib`
