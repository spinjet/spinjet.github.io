I am an aerospace engineer with a PhD from Cranfield University, specialising in **multidisciplinary design optimisation (MDO)** for hybrid-electric aircraft. My work sits at the intersection of aerodynamics, propulsion systems, and machine learning — finding efficient design solutions in large, complex design spaces.

After years of academic research, I am now transitioning into industry roles in **data science** and **systems engineering**, particularly within clean aviation and sustainable propulsion.

## What I Do

- **Aircraft Design Optimisation** — Set-based MDO, Pareto front exploration, surrogate modelling
- **CFD & Aerodynamics** — RANS simulations, airfoil optimisation, OpenFOAM / ANSYS
- **Machine Learning** — Gaussian processes, Bayesian networks, causal discovery, VAEs
- **Open-Source Development** — [PDOPT](https://github.com/spinjet/PDOPT) library for design optimisation
